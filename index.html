505PPT-Welcome to Paradise is a beachfront property located in Port Aransas, 5 km from Port Aransas Marina and 6 km from Port Aransas Nature Preserve. The air-conditioned accommodation is 5 km from The University of Texas Marine Science Institute, and guests benefit from complimentary WiFi and private parking available on site. The holiday home features 4 bedrooms, a TV with cable channels, an equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave, and 3 bathrooms with a bath or shower. Port Aransas Art Center is 3.1 km from the holiday home, while Port Aransas Museum is 3.5 km away.
